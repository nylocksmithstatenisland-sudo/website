Common Trunk Lockout Scenarios We Handle

🔑 Keys Locked in Trunk

Your car keys are locked inside the trunk. We can safely access the trunk to retrieve your keys without damaging your vehicle.

🚗 Trunk Release Button Inaccessible

Can't access the trunk release button inside your vehicle. Modern vehicles have electronic trunk releases that require alternative access methods.

🔄 Mechanical Lock Failure

The trunk lock mechanism has failed. Could be due to wear, corrosion, or mechanical damage requiring professional repair.

🔋 Power Trunk Malfunction

Electronic power trunk system not working. Could be electrical issues, fuse problems, or control module failures.

❄️ Frozen Lock Mechanism

Cold weather has frozen the trunk lock. Requires specialized techniques to safely thaw and access without damage.

🔐 Child Safety Lock Engaged

Child safety locks prevent trunk access from inside. Need professional assistance to safely access the trunk area.
