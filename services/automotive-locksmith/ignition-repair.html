Common Ignition Problems We Fix

🔑 Key Won't Turn

Key stuck in ignition or won't turn. Often caused by worn tumblers or steering wheel lock engagement.

🚗 Car Won't Start

Engine cranks but won't start, or no response when turning the key. Could be faulty ignition switch or wiring.

🔄 Intermittent Starting

Car starts sometimes but not others. Usually indicates a failing ignition switch or poor electrical connections.

🔋 No Electrical Response

Key turns but nothing happens - no lights, no sound. Typically a failed ignition switch or wiring issue.

🔐 Key Stuck in Ignition

Can't remove key from ignition. Often related to shifter mechanism or worn ignition cylinder.

⚠️ Warning Lights

Security or check engine lights illuminated. May indicate ignition system faults or security system issues.

Ignition System Components We Repair

🔑 Ignition Cylinder

The mechanical lock cylinder where you insert the key

🔄 Ignition Switch

Electrical switch that sends power to start the vehicle

🗝️ Tumblers

Internal pins that must align for the key to turn

🔌 Wiring Harness

Electrical connections between ignition components

🎛️ Steering Lock

Anti-theft mechanism that locks the steering wheel

🔐 Immobilizer

Electronic anti-theft system that prevents starting

📻 Transponder

Chip in the key that communicates with the immobilizer

🔋 Backup Systems

Redundant starting systems for modern vehicles
