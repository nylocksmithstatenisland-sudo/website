Safe Security Tips & Best Practices

📝 Record Keeping

Keep your safe combination and keys in a secure location separate from the safe. Consider using a trusted family member or attorney as backup.

🔄 Regular Testing

Test your safe combination regularly to ensure it works properly and haven't forgotten the combination due to lack of use.

📍 Strategic Placement

Position your safe in a secure location away from windows and exterior walls. Consider hiding it behind artwork or furniture.

🔐 Dual Access

Consider safes with dual access features allowing multiple authorized users to access contents without sharing the full combination.

🛡️ Professional Installation

Always have your safe professionally installed and anchored to prevent theft. Improper installation can compromise security.

📞 Emergency Planning

Keep your locksmith's contact information readily available and consider having a backup safe opening service on speed dial.
