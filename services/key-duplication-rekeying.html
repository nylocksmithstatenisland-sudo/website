When Should You Rekey Your Locks?

🏠 Moving to a New Home

Always rekey locks when moving into a new home. You never know how many copies of the previous owner's keys exist.

🗝️ Lost or Stolen Keys

If your keys are lost or stolen, rekeying provides immediate security by making old keys useless.

👥 Adding/Removing Tenants

Rekey when tenants move in or out to ensure only authorized individuals have access.

💼 Employee Changes

Rekey commercial locks when employees leave to prevent unauthorized access to your business.

🔐 Recent Break-in

After a break-in, rekeying ensures stolen keys cannot be used to access your property again.

📅 Regular Maintenance

Consider rekeying every few years as part of regular home security maintenance.
