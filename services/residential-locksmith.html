Home Security Tips from Staten Island Experts

🎯 Front Door Security

Your front door is your home's primary defense. Ensure it has a solid core door, quality deadbolt, and proper strike plate installation.

🔑 Key Management

Don't hide keys under mats or in obvious locations. Consider keyless entry systems or smart locks for better security.

💡 Lighting Matters

Well-lit exteriors deter burglars. Install motion-sensor lights around entrances and walkways.

🪟 Window Security

Secure all windows with proper locks. Consider window bars or security film for added protection.

🚪 Garage Safety

Don't forget your garage door. Ensure it has proper locks and consider automatic garage door openers with rolling codes.

📞 Emergency Planning

Have emergency contacts readily available and consider a home security system with monitoring services.
