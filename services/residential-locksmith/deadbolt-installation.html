Understanding ANSI Security Grades

The American National Standards Institute (ANSI) and Builders Hardware Manufacturers Association (BHMA) rate locks based on durability and security. Here's what each grade means for your home or business.

1

ANSI Grade 1

Commercial / Best Residential

  • Must withstand 250,000 cycles
  • Resists 10 door strikes with 75 lbs force
  • Highest security rating available

Examples: Schlage B662, Medeco Maxum, most high-security deadbolts

Recommended for commercial properties and high-security residential needs.

2

ANSI Grade 2

Exceeds Residential Standards

  • Must withstand 150,000 cycles
  • Resists 5 door strikes with 75 lbs force
  • Excellent for most homes

Examples: Schlage B60, Kwikset 980, most quality residential deadbolts

Our minimum recommendation for Staten Island homes. Best value for residential security.

3

ANSI Grade 3

Basic Residential

  • Must withstand 100,000 cycles
  • Resists 2 door strikes with 75 lbs force
  • Minimum acceptable standard

Examples: Budget deadbolts, builder-grade locks

Acceptable for basic security but we typically recommend upgrading to Grade 2 or Grade 1.

Our Recommendation for Staten Island:

We recommend Grade 2 minimum for most Staten Island homes, and Grade 1 for:

  • High-value homes or properties with valuable contents
  • Areas with higher crime rates
  • Commercial properties and businesses
  • Properties requiring insurance-approved security

Common Deadbolt Problems We Solve

Experienced problems with your deadbolt? We fix all types of deadbolt issues and can upgrade to a better solution if needed.

🔄 Misalignment with Strike Plate

Symptoms: Bolt won't extend fully, difficult to lock, requires force

Our Solution: Realign strike plate, adjust door hinges, reinforce frame if needed

🔑 Key Difficult to Turn

Symptoms: Key sticks, requires excessive force, won't turn smoothly

Our Solution: Clean mechanism, lubricate properly, rekey or replace if worn

⚙️ Bolt Sticks or Jams

Symptoms: Bolt won't extend or retract smoothly, gets stuck halfway

Our Solution: Clean and lubricate, replace internal mechanisms if damaged

❌ DIY Installation Gone Wrong

Symptoms: Wrong hole size, misalignment, gaps, doesn't work properly

Our Solution: Repair door damage, patch incorrect holes, reinstall professionally

Experiencing deadbolt problems? We can diagnose and fix the issue or recommend a better solution.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does deadbolt installation take?

Standard installation with existing holes takes 30-45 minutes. New installation requiring drilling takes 60-90 minutes. Multiple deadbolts can be installed in the same visit for efficiency.

Can you install a deadbolt on a metal door?

Yes! We have specialized tools and expertise for metal, fiberglass, and wood doors. Metal doors require different drilling techniques and we're experienced with all door materials common in Staten Island homes.

What's the best deadbolt brand?

For most Staten Island homes, we recommend Schlage for the best balance of security, durability, and value. For maximum security, Medeco is unmatched. For budget-conscious or rental properties, Kwikset with SmartKey is excellent.

Do I need a single or double cylinder deadbolt?

Single cylinder (key outside, thumb-turn inside) is standard and safest for most doors. Double cylinder (key both sides) is only recommended for doors with glass within 40" of the lock, but check local fire codes as they may restrict double cylinder use for safety reasons.

Can you install a smart deadbolt?

Absolutely! We install all major smart deadbolt brands including Schlage Encode, Kwikset Halo, Yale Assure, and more. We also program them and show you how to use the app. See our smart lock installation page for more details.

Will installation damage my door?

Professional installation should never damage your door. We use proper tools, precise measurements, and careful technique. If your door was previously damaged by DIY attempts, we can repair it during installation.

Do you provide deadbolts or do I buy them?

Either way! We can supply the deadbolt (we stock all major brands) or install one you purchased. If you're unsure which to buy, we're happy to recommend the best option for your needs and budget.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes! We warranty our installation workmanship for 1 year. Deadbolt hardware is covered by the manufacturer's warranty (typically 1-3 years depending on brand). We'll help you with any warranty claims if needed.
