Common Door Lock Problems We Repair

🔑 Sticking Keys

Keys that won't turn smoothly or get stuck in the cylinder due to dirt, corrosion, or misalignment.

🔒 Loose Deadbolts

Deadbolts that don't extend fully or retract properly, leaving your door unsecured.

🚪 Wobbly Door Knobs

Door knobs that spin without engaging the lock mechanism or feel loose in the door.

🔓 Broken Lock Mechanisms

Internal lock components that have failed due to wear, damage, or manufacturing defects.

🦠 Corrosion & Rust

Locks damaged by moisture, salt air, or exposure to the elements, common in coastal areas.

🚪 Misaligned Hardware

Door hardware that doesn't align properly due to door settling or improper installation.
