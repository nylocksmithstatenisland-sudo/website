Professional Lock Rekeying Services

When you need to change who has access to your home without replacing the entire lock, lock rekeying is the perfect solution. Our professional rekeying service in Staten Island allows you to keep your existing high-quality locks while ensuring that old keys no longer work.

Rekeying is much more cost-effective than full lock replacement and maintains the integrity of your door hardware. Our certified locksmiths can rekey deadbolts, door knobs, lever handles, and other lock types quickly and securely. Whether you're moving into a new home, dealing with lost keys, or simply want to enhance your security, rekeying provides peace of mind without the expense of new locks.

Why Rekey Instead of Replace?

💰 Cost Effective

Rekeying costs significantly less than replacing locks, saving you money while maintaining security.

⚡ Quick & Convenient

Most rekeying jobs can be completed in under 30 minutes without damaging your door or hardware.

🔧 Preserves Quality

Keep your high-quality, custom locks instead of settling for cheaper replacement options.

🏠 Maintains Aesthetics

No need to change the appearance of your doors or match new hardware finishes.

🔒 Immediate Security

Old keys become useless immediately, providing instant enhanced security for your home.

🔄 Eco-Friendly

Reduce waste by reusing existing quality hardware instead of manufacturing new locks.

When Should You Rekey Your Locks?

🏠 Moving Into a New Home

Never trust that previous owners returned all keys. Rekeying ensures only you have access to your new home.

🔑 Lost or Stolen Keys

If keys are lost or stolen, rekeying prevents unauthorized access rather than waiting for potential break-ins.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Changes

After divorce, roommate changes, or when adult children move out - ensure only authorized people have keys.

🏢 Rental Property

Landlords should rekey between tenants to prevent old tenants from retaining unauthorized access.

🔐 Recent Break-In

After a break-in, rekeying provides peace of mind that intruders can't return with copied keys.

🛠️ Maintenance

Periodic rekeying as part of routine home maintenance and security upgrades.

Our Lock Rekeying Process

1

Assessment

Inspect your locks to determine compatibility and plan the rekeying process for each lock type.

2

Disassembly

Carefully remove the lock cylinder from the door without damaging the lock or door hardware.

3

Rekeying

Replace internal pins and springs to match your new key, ensuring old keys no longer work.

4

Testing

Reassemble and test the lock with new keys to ensure smooth operation and proper security.
