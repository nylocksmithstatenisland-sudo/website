Professional Lock Installation Services

Enhance your home's security with professional lock installation services in Staten Island. Our certified locksmiths provide expert installation of deadbolts, smart locks, door knobs, and other security hardware to protect your family and property.

We work with all major lock brands and offer both new installations and upgrades to existing systems. Our technicians ensure proper alignment, secure mounting, and smooth operation for maximum security and convenience. Whether you're moving into a new home or upgrading your current security, we provide reliable, professional installation services.

Lock Types We Install

🔐 Deadbolts

Single and double cylinder deadbolts for maximum security. Available in various finishes and styles.

🚪 Door Knobs & Levers

Entry and passage door hardware with keyed and non-keyed options for different room requirements.

📱 Smart Locks

Keyless entry systems with smartphone control, keypad access, and remote monitoring capabilities.

🔒 Mortise Locks

Heavy-duty mortise locksets for front doors and high-traffic areas requiring enhanced security.

🔑 Keypad Locks

Digital keypad entry systems with programmable codes for family and guest access.

🛡️ Security Hardware

Reinforced strike plates, security hinges, and other hardware to prevent forced entry.
