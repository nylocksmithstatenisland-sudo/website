Locked Out of Your Car? Here's What To Do

Stay calm. Follow these steps to get back in your vehicle quickly and safely. For a comprehensive guide on what to do when locked out of your car in Staten Island, including prevention tips, cost expectations, and how to choose the right professional, check out our detailed article.

1. Move to a Safe Location

If you're on a busy road or highway, move to a nearby parking lot or side street. Your safety and the safety of other drivers comes first.

2. Call Us Immediately

Dial (718) 831-6269. We'll ask for your location, vehicle type, and situation to dispatch the nearest locksmith.

3. Stay With Your Vehicle

If safe, remain near your car so our technician can find you quickly. Keep your phone handy for any updates.

4. Have Proof Ready

Have your driver's license, vehicle registration, or proof of ownership available when the technician arrives. This speeds up the process.

⚠️ What NOT To Do

  • Do NOT attempt to break a window — this causes damage and poses injury risk
  • Do NOT try to pick the lock yourself without proper tools
  • Do NOT leave children or pets unattended in the vehicle while seeking help
  • Do NOT use slim jims or improvised tools — you could damage the airbag system or paint
