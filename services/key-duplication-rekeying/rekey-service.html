Professional Lock Rekeying Services

Our rekey service in Staten Island changes the internal combination of your locks so that old keys no longer work, while new keys operate the locks perfectly. This is a cost-effective alternative to full lock replacement and provides immediate security enhancement for your home or business.

Rekeying is essential when you've lost keys, experienced a break-in, or simply want to change who has access to your property. Our certified locksmiths use precision tools to reconfigure the lock mechanism, ensuring that only authorized keys will open your doors. We work with all major lock brands and can rekey single locks or entire master key systems.

When Should You Rekey Your Locks?

🔑 Lost or Stolen Keys

If you've lost your keys or they were stolen, rekeying ensures the old keys can't be used to access your property.

🏠 Moving In/Out

When moving into a new home or renting out your property, rekeying provides fresh security.

🔒 Break-In Prevention

After a break-in or security incident, rekeying prevents the intruder from returning with copied keys.

👥 Key Sharing Concerns

If you've given keys to contractors, ex-employees, or others who no longer need access, rekeying revokes their access.

🔄 Master Key Changes

For commercial properties, rekeying can change master key combinations for enhanced security.

💰 Cost Effective Security

Rekeying is much more affordable than replacing all your locks while providing the same security benefits.

Rekeying vs. Lock Replacement

🔄 Rekeying Benefits

  • • Much more affordable than replacement
  • • Keeps your existing quality locks
  • • Faster service completion
  • • Maintains door hardware compatibility
  • • Preserves original lock appearance

⚡ Rekeying Process

  • • Remove lock from door
  • • Disassemble lock cylinder
  • • Replace internal pins and springs
  • • Reassemble with new combination
  • • Test with new keys

🔧 When to Replace

  • • Lock is physically damaged
  • • Lock is outdated or low-quality
  • • Upgrading to smart locks
  • • Changing door hardware
  • • Lock mechanism is worn out

💰 Cost Comparison

  • • Rekeying: $15-30 per lock
  • • Replacement: $75-200 per lock
  • • Rekeying saves 60-85%
  • • Same day service available
  • • No door hardware changes needed

Our Professional Rekeying Process

1

Lock Assessment

Evaluate the lock type, condition, and determine if rekeying is appropriate or if replacement is needed.

2

Lock Disassembly

Carefully remove the lock from the door and disassemble the cylinder to access the internal mechanism.

3

Pin Replacement

Replace the internal pins and springs with new components to create a fresh key combination.

4

Testing & Keys

Reassemble the lock, test functionality, and cut new keys that match the updated combination.
