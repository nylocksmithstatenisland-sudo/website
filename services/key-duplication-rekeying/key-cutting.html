Professional Key Cutting & Duplication Services

Our key cutting service in Staten Island provides fast, accurate duplication of all types of keys. Whether you need spare keys for your home, office, or vehicle, our professional locksmiths use state-of-the-art key cutting machines to ensure perfect copies that work reliably every time.

We cut keys for residential locks, commercial doors, automotive applications, and even specialty keys. Our precision equipment handles everything from standard house keys to complex high-security keys with laser-cut profiles. Available 24 hours a day, we provide same-day service for most key cutting needs throughout Staten Island.
