Automotive Security Tips from Staten Island Experts

🗝️ Spare Key Strategy

Keep a spare key in a magnetic key holder under your vehicle frame, not in your wallet or hidden in obvious places.

🔒 Anti-Theft Devices

Consider installing steering wheel locks, brake locks, or alarm systems as additional layers of vehicle protection.

📍 Parking Smart

Park in well-lit areas and avoid leaving valuables visible in your vehicle, even for short periods.

🔑 Key Fob Care

Replace key fob batteries every 1-2 years and avoid exposing them to extreme temperatures or moisture.

🚗 Regular Maintenance

Have your ignition system and locks inspected regularly to catch potential issues before they become emergencies.

📞 Emergency Plan

Keep your locksmith's number programmed in your phone and consider roadside assistance membership for peace of mind.
