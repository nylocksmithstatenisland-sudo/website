Common Safe Problems We Fix

🔑 Stuck Locks

Locks that won't turn or operate properly due to mechanical failure or damage.

🔢 Forgotten Combinations

Help recover or reset combinations when the original combination is lost or forgotten.

🔋 Dead Batteries

Electronic safes with dead batteries that prevent access to stored items.

💔 Broken Keys

Keys that break inside locks or keyholes, requiring extraction and repair.

🚪 Door Alignment Issues

Doors that won't close properly or have damaged hinges and latches.

🔧 Wear & Tear

General wear from regular use requiring component replacement and lubrication.

Safe Maintenance & Prevention

🛠️ Regular Maintenance Tasks

  • • Clean and lubricate lock mechanisms
  • • Test combination and key operation
  • • Inspect hinges and door alignment
  • • Check electronic components
  • • Replace batteries in electronic safes

🛡️ Preventive Measures

  • • Avoid slamming the safe door
  • • Keep safe away from moisture
  • • Use high-quality keys and combinations
  • • Regular professional inspections
  • • Proper storage of contents
