Why Regular Lock Maintenance Matters

Regular lock maintenance is crucial for maintaining security and preventing costly repairs. In Staten Island's variable climate, locks are exposed to moisture, temperature changes, and salt air that can cause premature wear. Our expert locksmiths recommend scheduling professional maintenance at least twice a year to keep your locks functioning smoothly and your property secure.
